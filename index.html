Strengthsfinder Assessment

We'll email you an access code and a link to take a Strengthsfinder quiz this week. Strengthsfinder is an assessment to help you identify which abilities you lead with as your personal strengths. The quiz generates a personalized insights report and an Action-Planning Guide. Completing the quiz and reading the results should take about 30 minutes. We're introducing you to Strenghtsfinder to accomplish two goals. First, it's a good way to self-reflect on your own work style, learn about your fellow students, and understand howothers prefer to tackle challenges within group environments. Second, it's an assessment that's widely used within hiring partner organizations, so being able to speak confidently to your Strengths results may help you within future job interviews.
